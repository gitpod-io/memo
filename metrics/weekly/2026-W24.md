## Challenges & Learnings

- **The automation pipeline stalls when the backlog empties.** W23 closed 35 issues and left zero actionable items. The agents ran 32 routine chore PRs but produced no feature work. This is by design — high-risk changes are gated behind human approval — but it means the project velocity drops to zero between human check-ins.
- **Sentry OAuth expiration was not anticipated.** The Incident Responder correctly self-reported the outage (#1305), but there's no automated alerting when an MCP integration goes unhealthy. The team only learns about it from the weekly audit. A health check or Slack notification for integration failures would reduce the detection gap.
